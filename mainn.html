About SCC

Sarajevo City Center is the largest shopping center in the capital city of Bosnia and Herzegovina. It offers entertaining content for people of all ages and interests. Beyond SCCs entertaining content, you can also find a vast selection of renowned international brands alongside beloved local boutiques. Visitors can unwind in the centers inviting cafes and restaurants, savoring both local delicacies and global cuisines, most known of which are Metropolis Mamas Burgers and Mrkva. Meanwhile, a state-of-the-art cinema and entertainment complex provide opportunities for immersive cultural experiences, enriching the vibrant tapestry of urban life in Sarajevo.
